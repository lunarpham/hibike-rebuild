INTRODUCTION

Established in 2015, over 8 year of services, Hibike has up to 10 stores located in 7 countries and regions in the South-East Asia region and Hong Kong/Taiwan, bringing our passion for music and anime to customers all over. We are honored to be considered as the most favorite instrument reseller among customers in the South-East Asia region, thanks to our exceptional customer service and extensive collection of musical instruments.
Hibike is established by inspiration from our loves to TV series anime "Hibike! Euphonium" and others music anime titles. In Japanese, "hibike" is verb that means "make sounds" in English, and our name represents our commitment to providing high-quality instruments and equipment that allow musicians to make beautiful sounds.
Hibike are proud to partner with some of the most renowned and reputable brands in the industry, including Yamaha, Epiphone, Steinberg, and Marshall. These brands have a long-standing history of producing high-quality instruments, amplifiers, and recording equipment, which have been used by some of the biggest names in the music industry.
