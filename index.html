ABOUT US

Inspiration

Hibike is established by inspiration from our loves to TV series anime "Hibike! Euphonium". At Hibike, we believe that music has the power to inspire, and we are proud to be a part of the journey that musicians take to find their voice and sound.

Development

Established in 2015, over 8 year of services, Hibike has up to 10 stores located in 7 countries/regions in South-East Asia and Hong Kong/Taiwan, bringing our passion for music and anime to customers all over.

Reputation

Hibike are proud to partner with some of the most renowned and reputable brands in the industry, including Yamaha, Epiphone, Boss, and Marshall.
