WHO ARE US?

I - FIRST SPEECH

Welcome to Hibike, the ultimate musical instrument stores with a twist of Japanese anime culture! With our unique theme, we offer a one-of-a-kind shopping experience that is both fun and exciting. Established in 2015, over 8 year of services, Hibike has up to 10 stores located in 7 countries/regions, bringing our passion for music and anime to customers all over. We are honored to be considered as the most favorite instrument reseller among customers in the South-East Asia and Hong Kong/Taiwan, thanks to our exceptional customer service and extensive collection of musical instruments. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced musician, we have a wide selection of instruments to choose from, especially if you are looking for what instrument your beloved anime characters play. We pride ourselves on providing high-quality products and ensuring that every customer finds what they are looking for. At Hibike, we not only sell instruments but also offer repair services, music lessons, and performance opportunities. Come and explore our store, where music and anime meet to create a unique and unforgettable experience!

II - OUR BRAND NAME AND SLOGAN

Hibike is established by inspiration from our loves to TV series anime "Hibike! Euphonium" and others music anime titles. In Japanese, "hibike" is a verb that literally means "make sounds" in English, and our name represents our commitment to providing high-quality instruments and equipment that allow musicians to make beautiful sounds. Our slogan, "The way you sound it", is a reflection of our belief that every musician has their unique sound, and our goal is to help them achieve it with our products and services. By choosing the name Hibike as our store name and incorporating it into our slogan, we are emphasizing our dedication to music and the art of creating beautiful sounds. We want to be the go-to destination for musicians looking to find the perfect instruments and equipment to achieve their sound. At Hibike, we believe that music has the power to inspire, and we are proud to be a part of the journey that musicians take to find their voice and sound.
